Five chapters in, the Monumental Experience series continues to evolve.

Creatures Across the World builds on the core Monumental Experience foundation and introduces Cobblemon & the Aether to expand how players explore and interact with the world.

Biomes are designed to feel distinct and purposeful, with creatures spawning naturally based on their environment. Exploration leads to meaningful encounters, new discoveries, and multiple paths of progression across the world.

The modpack moves beyond basic survival and linear progression, emphasizing exploration, collection, and long-term world development while allowing players to progress at their own pace.

⚔️ Explore diverse and challenging biomes
🧭 Discover creatures across varied environments
🏗️ Build, survive, and develop your world
✨ The next evolution of the Monumental Experience series

A familiar foundation, expanded with new depth and life.
